1st Apperance

The rocks first match was under the name of Rocky Maivia in 1996’s survivor series november. But he was previously seen as part of the entourage of Marc Mero just weeks before his first match. The match was a elimination match where Rocky was the sole survivor for his team after he had pinned crush and goldust. The match lasted 23 minutes and 44 seconds where it was finished with a pin on goldust after a shoulder breaker which at the time was widely known to be Rocky Maivia finishing move .

Title matches

His first title match was in 1997 where he had a fight on monday night raw against the then known Hunter Hearst Helmsley (aka triple H) where he had won the intercontinental championship. Later he had his first fight in In Your House: 13 in which he had defended the title against Hunter Hearst Helmsley. Wrestlemania 13 followed where Rocky maivia defended the title yet again against The Sultan with another successful defense of the title under his belt. He would defend the title a further 2 times with the last defense the fans around the stadium were heard shouting chants such as “Die, Rocky, die!”. He then lost the championship against owen hart on april 28 1997 on a episode of raw is war.

WWE Startup

Dwayne had a big year prior to the WWF name change to WWE where he had a match against Hulk Hogan which was dubbed the icon vs icon match. Where the Rock was the victor metaphorically passing on the torch from one generation to the next. When the wrestling company was hit with a lawsuit from the WWF nature foundation forcing the company to change there name to WWE. The rock was one of the first wrestler to hold the wwe championship title as the WWF changed its name to the WWE in 2002 (the rock was the third wrestler to hold the newly named title after Hulk Hogan and The Undertaker). The rock later lost the title in the span of a month to Brock Lesnar. After the loss The Rock declared that whether or not the crowd booed him he would always be the people’s champion.

Retirement

With The Rock’s wrestling career taking a backseat to Dwayne’s acting career there were fewer appearances until 2003 where The Rock returned to face Hulk Hogan re establishing himself as a heel for the first time since 1999. With a draft to raw Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson had a failing attempt to win the World Heavyweight Championship he turned his attention to the long lasting feud with Steve Austin after he was chosen as the “superstar of the decade” this feud lead to a match at wrestlemania where The Rock won the match (this match later turned out to be steve cold’s last match). The next night was dubbed as the rocks appreciation night where he was attacked by the newcomer that was Goldberg. The Rock then had the last match as a active wrestler against Goldberg.

the Return

With Dwayne’s acting picking up more and more traction wrestling became a more and more scares appearance that was up until 2011 where The Rock was announced to be the host of wrestlemania XXVII with an appearance on Raw for the first time in 7 years. In a lengthy promo the famous quote of john cena being a big fat bowl of fruity pebbles was made with fans taking to it with signs and chants for cena. After a year of promos and feud developments against cena and the miz dwayne fought against john cena at wrestlemania for a win that in The Rock's own words cemented him as the best wrestler of all time. The Rock would then continue to make appearances over 2 years where he would then defend his WWE championship against John Cena at Wrestlemania 29 where he ultimately lost ending his 70 day reign of the title this would later be seen as his last match of the year due to a torn ligament. With further scatter appearances until 2016 Rock’s last match was in Wrestlemania where he had a impromptu match against the wyatt family where he pinned Brad Wyatt in 6 seconds making it the fastest win of wrestlemania history cementing himself in the wrestlemania history books.
